## Meagan Rodriguez is a musician & filmmaker.

In 2014, she graduated with a BFA in Film & Television from New York University's Tisch School of the Arts with a specialization in post production and sound design.

Combining both her love for film and music, Meagan has worked as a composer, sound designer, as well as a post production consultant on films, television and branded content.

Recently she’s had the opportunity to tour internationally with her personal music project, [QRTR](https://www.qrtr.party/). The release of her second album [infina ad nausea](https://qrtr.bandcamp.com/album/infina-ad-nausea) described by Pitchfork as an “euphoric trip through the mind” filled with “alternative-universe club classics in pastel blues and sludgy blacks” (Paste Magazine) has led her to performing on stages ranging from Coachella to Red Rocks.

She loves port wine and will gladly read your tarot.
